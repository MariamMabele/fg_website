Contact · Help · Support

We would love to hear from you.

If you need help, want to reach Frappe Girls HQ, contact local organizers, or ask a general question, start here. Check the quick answers first, then send your message below.

Before you send a message

How do I apply for a workshop?

Go to the Events page and use the workshop application form for the event you want to attend.

I want to organize an event.

Start from the Organize page. It explains the process and gives you the organizer request form.

I want to coach or sponsor.

You can still contact us here, but the Coach and Sponsors pages already provide the best path for those requests.

Who should I choose in the form?

Choose HQ for platform-wide questions, Local Organizer for event-specific help, and General if you are not sure where your message belongs.

Send your message

Fill in your name, email, contact target, and message. Once submitted, your request will be emailed to the Frappe Girls team.
