Coach · Support · Community

Help learners move forward as a coach.

Coaches are at the heart of every Frappe Girls event. They support participants during the workshop, help solve problems, answer questions, and make the day feel welcoming and practical.

What coaches do

Coaches guide small groups, help with setup and troubleshooting, explain concepts in a calm way, and encourage participants when they feel stuck.

Who can coach

Coaches can be Frappe developers, ERPNext consultants, experienced ERPNext users, accountants, operators, or professionals who understand the workflows well enough to support learners.

Ready to volunteer as a coach?

If you want to support workshops and help new learners feel confident with Frappe and ERPNext, submit your coaching interest form.
