Business technology is for every woman who runs one.

Frappe Girls is a non-profit community helping women understand, use, and build on open-source business technology, especially Frappe Framework and ERPNext.

What is Frappe Girls?

We organize free, one-day workshops where participants set up their first ERPNext environment, explore DocTypes, understand real business workflows, and learn with support from experienced coaches.

East Africa Launch region

Our Vision

Every woman who runs a business, manages accounts, or works in operations should have access to powerful open-source tools and the confidence to use them. Frappe Girls exists to make that practical, visible, and community-supported.

How it works

The workshop journey is simple: apply, build, and continue learning through the wider community.

01

Apply

Participants apply for a free workshop. No prior programming experience is required, only curiosity and a laptop.

02

Build

In one practical workshop day, participants learn the basics of Frappe and ERPNext, create records, and understand live workflows with coach support.

03

Grow

After the workshop, learners continue through future events, contribution paths, the Frappe tutorial, and the wider coaching and organizer community.
